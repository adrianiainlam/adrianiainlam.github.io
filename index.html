Some Greasemonkey scripts I've written:

  • SoundCloud download - Download mp3 from SoundCloud
  • Fake news alert - Alert when visiting a site with potential fake news
  • 9gag NSFW loader - Loads 9gag NSFW posts without login
  • zh.wikipedia locale switcher - Automatically select zh-HK locale
  • Show SMBC aftercomic - When you are too lazy to move your cursor
  • xkcd keyboard nav - Navigate xkcd with your keyboard

To install these scripts, install Greasemonkey (Firefox) or Tampermonkey (Chrome), then go to the link above and press "Raw".
